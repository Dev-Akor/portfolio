## Overview

The Kira Scales website is the official digital presence for Kira Scales Limited, our industrial weighing solutions company. It needed to communicate professionalism, technical expertise, and trustworthiness to potential clients in the B2B space.

## Requirements

The site needed to:

- Showcase our products (weighbridges, truck scales, floor scales, platform scales)
- Detail our services (installation, calibration, maintenance)
- Generate qualified leads through contact forms
- Rank well for relevant search terms in Nigeria
- Work reliably on slower internet connections

## Design Decisions

### Technology Choice

I chose Next.js with static generation for maximum performance and reliability. In Nigeria, CDN-delivered static pages load faster and are more resilient to server issues.

```
Performance Goals:
- Lighthouse Score: 95+
- First Contentful Paint: < 1.5s
- Time to Interactive: < 2.5s
- Core Web Vitals: All green
```

### Content Strategy

Content is managed via Contentlayer with MDX files. This allows:
- Version-controlled content
- Rich text with embedded components
- No database dependency
- Easy updates without deployment

### Lead Generation

The contact form integrates with:
- Email notifications for immediate response
- Structured data capture for follow-up
- WhatsApp link for Nigerian business culture (where WhatsApp is primary business communication)

## Key Features

### Product Catalog

Each product page includes:
- Technical specifications
- Typical applications
- High-quality imagery
- Request-a-quote CTA

### Service Pages

Services are detailed with:
- Process explanation
- What's included
- Timeline expectations
- Pricing approach (quote-based)

### SEO

Targeted local SEO for terms like:
- "weighbridge installation Nigeria"
- "truck scale Lagos"
- "weighbridge calibration"
- "industrial scales Nigeria"

## Results

Since launch:
- 40% of new inquiries come through the website
- Average page load time < 2 seconds
- Top 5 Google ranking for "weighbridge Nigeria"
- Consistent qualified leads monthly

## What I'd Do Differently

With more time, I'd add:
- Customer portal for service records and calibration certificates
- Online specification configurator
- Case studies from existing clients
- Live chat integration

The website has proven that even in B2B industrial markets, a well-executed digital presence generates measurable business impact.
